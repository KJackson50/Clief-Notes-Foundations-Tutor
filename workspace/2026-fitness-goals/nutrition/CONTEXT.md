# nutrition

## What this workspace is for

This is where you will go to create a nutrition plan for the user.
Thsi will include quick meals on the fly, or week long meal plans.
The constraints are 2000 calories a day.

## Process

You will take the users prompt or file, scour local .mds or the internet, the use the info to do a meal plan.

## What files live here

recipes, ingredients, and plans will live here. They will all me in .md format.
